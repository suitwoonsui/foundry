# Pillars, paths, and the funding page

**Six Pillars of the Foundry** is the umbrella: six areas where the community holds the project up. Within each pillar, people progress on a **path** of earned recognition (Heralds, Council, Orators, Diplomats, Forge, Vault). This doc covers culture, recognition levels, where funders fit, and the funding page as entry gate. Previous: [01-core-concept-wallets-and-layout.md](01-core-concept-wallets-and-layout.md). Next: [03-path-naming-options.md](03-path-naming-options.md).

Perfect. So what you have is not a single straight-line ladder.
It's roles based on how someone contributes — not how much they contribute.
This is important because it means:
Someone who raids like a demon is just as valuable as someone who donates funds.


Someone whose superpower is design, consulting, hosting calls, networking should not be forced into the same lane as someone who wants to supply liquidity or cover DEX boosts.


Identity grows through contribution style, not rank grinding.


So instead of pay-to-rank tiers, we use **Six Pillars of the Foundry** as the high-level frame, and **paths** for how someone actually contributes and levels up. Each pillar is a domain of contribution (social, strategy, voice, partnerships, build, fuel). Each named path—The Heralds, The Council, The Orators, The Diplomats, The Forge, The Vault—is how you show up inside that pillar, with earned recognition levels along the way.
This lets people belong to something that fits who they already are.

So where do funders fit?
Funding is one path (The Vault), equal to others.
This prevents:
Elites


Whales controlling direction


Pay-to-win culture


Resentment between non-funders and funders


Someone who funds is simply someone whose contribution form is fuel, not time.
They do not have more governance power.
They do not outrank others by default.
They are respected as one contributor type among many.
Perfect alignment with:
Team drives. Community fuels.

Now, the funding page fits in naturally
The funding page is not "please donate."
It is:
Choose how you fuel the engine.
Where the contribution options include:
Raid campaigns (earn)


Build tools or content (create)


Host AMAs or bring partners (connect)


Or fund a milestone (fuel)


This page becomes the entry gate to choosing a path.
Someone who contributes funding is on the Vault path, just as someone who raids progresses on the Heralds path.
No hierarchy.
Just different forms of strength.
